My Campaign/My Tenure App

The MyCampaign/MyTenure App is designed to enable politicians reach fast-paced and on-the-go voters who are need of legit and up-to-date information on their prospective candidates. Useful resources are mae available to constituents, no matter the demographic. Citizens can now have speedy access to view and assess political campaigns.

Reminderz

Timeliness is the wing upon which most businesses operate. Reminderz is an appointment scheduling and management application. This application was initially developed for health care centres but is now also adaptable by other businesses around the world.

IP Camera Installation

With the state of insecurity all over the world, it has become pertinent to have smart means of keeping your house or environments safe. The IP Cameras we install are state of the art digital video camera that receives control data and sends image data via an IP network. They are commonly used for surveillance but unlike analog closed-circuit television (CCTV) cameras, they require no local recording device, only a local area network.

Access Door Installation

We strive to use innovations in technology to ensure our clients are safe. Our Access doors installation process includes a thoroughly tested approach to ensure that break-ins are down to zero.
