Who We Are

Who We Are

Aymzar Solutions is an IT Solutions provider that was started in 2010 by a group of individuals. On relocating back to Nigeria having gained experience in various multinational corporations that includes; Virgin Media, Fujitsu, Vodafone and Logica CMG, they saw a huge gap in the market for IT support and solutions for local businesses and homes. At that time, there weren’t many organizations offering support solutions to these sectors; those that were, were often of poor quality. Due to lack of qualified regulatory authorities in the industry, they soon realized that it was important to build a name that people could trust and rely on - Aymzar Solutions was born.
